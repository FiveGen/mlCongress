H. Res. 466

                In the House of Representatives, U. S.,

                                                     November 17, 2011.
    Resolved, That it shall be in order at any time through the legislative day 
of November 18, 2011, for the Speaker to entertain motions that the House 
suspend the rules, as though under clause 1 of rule XV, relating to the joint 
resolution (H.J. Res. 2) proposing a balanced budget amendment to the 
Constitution of the United States. Debate on such a motion shall be extended to 
five hours.
    Sec. 2.  The Chair may postpone further consideration of a motion considered 
pursuant to this resolution to such time as may be designated by the Speaker.
            Attest:

                                                                          Clerk.
