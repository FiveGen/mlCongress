                                 AN ACT


 
 To name the Department of Veterans Affairs medical center in Spokane, 
  Washington, as the ``Mann-Grandstaff Department of Veterans Affairs 
                           Medical Center''.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. NAME OF DEPARTMENT OF VETERANS AFFAIRS MEDICAL CENTER, 
              SPOKANE, WASHINGTON.

    The Department of Veterans Affairs medical center in Spokane, 
Washington, shall after the date of the enactment of this Act be known 
and designated as the ``Mann-Grandstaff Department of Veterans Affairs 
Medical Center''. Any reference to such medical center in any law, 
regulation, map, document, record, or other paper of the United States 
shall be considered to be a reference to the Mann-Grandstaff Department 
of Veterans Affairs Medical Center.

            Passed the House of Representatives December 20, 2012.
