                          Concurrent Resolution

    Resolved by the Senate (the House of Representatives concurring), 
That in recognition of the long and distinguished service rendered to 
the Nation by Daniel K. Inouye, a Senator from the State of Hawaii and 
formerly a Representative from that State, his remains be permitted to 
lie in state in the rotunda of the Capitol on December 20, 2012, and 
the Architect of the Capitol, under the direction of the Speaker of the 
House of Representatives and the President pro tempore of the Senate, 
shall take all necessary steps for the accomplishment of that purpose.
Attest:

                                               Secretary of the Senate.
