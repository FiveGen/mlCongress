                               RESOLUTION


 
 Permitting the solicitation of donations in Senate buildings for the 
                 relief of victims of Superstorm Sandy.

    Resolved,

SECTION 1. SOLICITATION FOR SUPERSTORM SANDY RELIEF.

    Notwithstanding any other provision of the rules or regulations of 
the Senate--
            (1) a Senator, officer of the Senate, or employee of the 
        Senate may solicit another Senator, officer of the Senate, or 
        employee of the Senate within Senate buildings for nonmonetary 
        donations for the relief of victims of Superstorm Sandy during 
        the 30-day period beginning on the date on which the Senate 
        agrees to this resolution; and
            (2) a Senator, officer of the Senate, or employee of the 
        Senate may work with a nonprofit organization with respect to 
        the delivery of donations described in paragraph (1).
