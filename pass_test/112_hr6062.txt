                                 AN ACT


 
   To reauthorize the Edward Byrne Memorial Justice Assistance Grant 
                   Program through fiscal year 2017.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. SHORT TITLE.

    This Act may be cited as the ``Edward Byrne Memorial Justice 
Assistance Grant Program Reauthorization Act of 2012''.

SEC. 2. REAUTHORIZATION OF BYRNE JAG GRANTS.

    Section 508 of title I of the Omnibus Crime Control and Safe 
Streets Act of 1968 (42 U.S.C. 3758) is amended by inserting before the 
period the following: ``, and $800,000,000 for each of the fiscal years 
2013 through 2017''.
