H. Res. 600

                In the House of Representatives, U. S.,

                                                        March 29, 2012.
    Resolved, That upon the adoption of this resolution it shall be in order to 
consider in the House the bill (H.R. 4281) to provide an extension of Federal-
aid highway, highway safety, motor carrier safety, transit, and other programs 
funded out of the Highway Trust Fund pending enactment of a multiyear law 
reauthorizing such programs. All points of order against consideration of the 
bill are waived. The bill shall be considered as read. All points of order 
against provisions in the bill are waived. The previous question shall be 
considered as ordered on the bill and any amendment thereto to final passage 
without intervening motion except: (1) one hour of debate equally divided and 
controlled by the chair and ranking minority member of the Committee on 
Transportation and Infrastructure; and (2) one motion to recommit.
    Sec. 2.  The requirement of clause 6(a) of rule XIII for a two-thirds vote 
to consider a report from the Committee on Rules on the same day it is presented 
to the House is waived with respect to any resolution reported on the 
legislative day of March 29, 2012, providing for consideration or disposition of 
a measure extending expiring surface transportation authority.
