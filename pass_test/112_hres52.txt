H. Res. 52

                In the House of Representatives, U. S.,

                                                      January 25, 2011.
    Resolved, That the following named Members be, and are hereby, elected to 
the following standing committees of the House of Representatives:
    Committee on the Budget: Ms. Schwartz, Ms. Kaptur, Mr. Doggett, Mr. 
Blumenauer, Ms. McCollum, Mr. Yarmuth, Mr. Pascrell, Mr. Honda, Mr. Ryan of 
Ohio, Ms. Wasserman Schultz, Ms. Moore, Ms. Castor of Florida, Mr. Shuler, Mr. 
Tonko, and Ms. Bass of California.
    Committee on Foreign Affairs: Ms. Schwartz, to rank immediately after Mr. 
Higgins.
    Committee on House Administration: Ms. Zoe Lofgren of California and Mr. 
Gonzalez.
    Committee on Veterans' Affairs: Ms. Linda T. Sanchez of California, to rank 
immediately after Mr. Michaud.
