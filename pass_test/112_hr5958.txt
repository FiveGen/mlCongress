                                 AN ACT


 
To name the Jamaica Bay Wildlife Refuge Visitor Contact Station of the 
Jamaica Bay Wildlife Refuge unit of Gateway National Recreation Area in 
                       honor of James L. Buckley.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. NAMING OF JAMAICA BAY WILDLIFE REFUGE VISITOR CONTACT 
              STATION, JAMAICA BAY WILDLIFE REFUGE UNIT, GATEWAY 
              NATIONAL RECREATION AREA.

    (a) Naming.--The Jamaica Bay Wildlife Refuge Visitor Contact 
Station of the Jamaica Bay Wildlife Refuge unit of Gateway National 
Recreation Area in the State of New York shall be known and designated 
as the ``James L. Buckley Visitor Contact Station''.
    (b) References.--Any reference in any statute, rule, regulation, 
Executive order, publication, map, paper, or other document of the 
United States to the facility referred to in subsection (a) shall be 
deemed to be a reference to the ``James L. Buckley Visitor Contact 
Station''.
