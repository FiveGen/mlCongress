                               RESOLUTION


 
         To make effective appointment of Senate Legal Counsel.

    Resolved, That the appointment of Morgan J. Frankel of the District 
of Columbia to be Senate Legal Counsel, made by the President pro 
tempore this day, shall become effective as of January 7, 2011, and the 
term of service of the appointee shall expire at the end of the One 
Hundred Thirteenth Congress.
