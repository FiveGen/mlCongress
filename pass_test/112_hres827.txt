H. Res. 827

                In the House of Representatives, U. S.,

                                                     December 12, 2012.
    Resolved, That it shall be in order at any time through the legislative day 
of December 28, 2012, for the Speaker to entertain motions that the House 
suspend the rules as though under clause 1 of rule XV. The Speaker or his 
designee shall consult with the Minority Leader or her designee on the 
designation of any matter for consideration pursuant to this resolution.
