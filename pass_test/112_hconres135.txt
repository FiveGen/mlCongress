                          Concurrent Resolution

    Resolved by the House of Representatives (the Senate concurring),

SECTION 1. USE OF ROTUNDA FOR PRESENTATION OF CONGRESSIONAL GOLD MEDAL 
              TO DAW AUNG SAN SUU KYI.

    The Rotunda of the Capitol is authorized to be used on September 
19, 2012, for the presentation of the Congressional Gold Medal to Daw 
Aung San Suu Kyi, in recognition of her leadership and perseverance in 
the struggle for freedom and democracy in Burma. Physical preparations 
for the ceremony shall be carried out in accordance with such 
conditions as the Architect of the Capitol may prescribe.
