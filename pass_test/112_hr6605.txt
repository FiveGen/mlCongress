                                 AN ACT


 
 To eliminate an unnecessary reporting requirement for an unfunded DNA 
                     Identification grant program.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. ELIMINATION OF REPORT REQUIREMENT.

    Section 2406 of title I of the Omnibus Crime Control and Safe 
Streets Act of 1968 (42 U.S.C. 3796kk-5) is repealed.

            Passed the House of Representatives December 5, 2012.
