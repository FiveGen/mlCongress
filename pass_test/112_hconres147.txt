H. CON. RES. 147

                         CONCURRENT RESOLUTION

    Resolved by the House of Representatives (the Senate concurring), 
That pursuant to the last sentence of section 106 of title 1, United 
States Code, the requirement of section 107 of such title that the 
enrollment of any bill or joint resolution be printed on parchment is 
waived for the duration of the One Hundred Twelfth Congress, and the 
enrollment of any such bill or joint


              

 resolution shall be in such form as may be certified by the Clerk of 
the House of Representatives or the Secretary of the Senate (as 
applicable) to be a truly enrolled bill or joint resolution (as the 
case may be).

            Passed the House of Representatives January 1, 2013.

            Attest:

                                                                 Clerk.
