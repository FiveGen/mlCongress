H. Res. 355

                In the House of Representatives, U. S.,

                                                         July 19, 2011.
    Resolved, That upon the adoption of this resolution it shall be in order to 
consider in the House the bill (H.R. 2560) to cut, cap, and balance the Federal 
budget. All points of order against consideration of the bill are waived. The 
bill shall be considered as read. All points of order against provisions in the 
bill are waived. The previous question shall be considered as ordered on the 
bill to final passage without intervening motion except: (1) four hours of 
debate equally divided and controlled by the chair and ranking minority member 
of the Committee on the Budget; and (2) one motion to recommit.
            Attest:

                                                                          Clerk.
