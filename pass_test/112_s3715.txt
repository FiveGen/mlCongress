                                 A BILL


 
To extend the limited antitrust exemption contained in the Pandemic and 
                     All-Hazards Preparedness Act.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. EXTENSION OF LIMITED ANTITRUST EXEMPTION.

    Section 405(b) of the Pandemic and All-Hazards Preparedness Act (42 
U.S.C. 247d-6a note) is amended by striking ``6-year'' and inserting 
``7-year''.
