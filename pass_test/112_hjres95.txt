        H.J.Res.95

                      One Hundred Twelfth Congress

                                 of the

                        United States of America


                          AT THE FIRST SESSION

         Begun and held at the City of Washington on Wednesday,
            the fifth day of January, two thousand and eleven


                            Joint Resolution


 
 Making further continuing appropriations for fiscal year 2012, and for 
                             other purposes.

    Resolved by the Senate and House of Representatives of the United 
States of America in Congress assembled, That the Continuing 
Appropriations Act, 2012 (Public Law 112-36) is further amended by 
striking the date specified in section 106(3) and inserting ``December 
23, 2011''.

                               Speaker of the House of Representatives.

                            Vice President of the United States and    
                                               President of the Senate.
