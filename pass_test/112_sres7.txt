                               RESOLUTION


 
     To make effective appointment of Deputy Senate Legal Counsel.

    Resolved, That the appointment of Patricia Mack Bryan of Virginia 
to be Deputy Senate Legal Counsel, made by the President pro tempore 
this day, shall become effective as of January 3, 2011, and the term of 
service of the appointee shall expire at the end of the One Hundred 
Thirteenth Congress.
