H. Res. 515

                In the House of Representatives, U. S.,

                                                      January 18, 2012.
    Resolved, That a motion to proceed with regard to a joint resolution of 
disapproval specified in subsection (a)(2) of section 3101A of title 31, United 
States Code--
     (a) may be offered even if the joint resolution has not been reported to 
the House as contemplated by subsection (c)(3) of such section; and
    (b) shall be in order only if offered by the Majority Leader or his 
designee.
            Attest:

                                                                          Clerk.
