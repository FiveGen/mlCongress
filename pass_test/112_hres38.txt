H. Res. 38

                In the House of Representatives, U. S.,

                                                      January 25, 2011.
    Resolved, That, pursuant to section 3(b)(1) of House Resolution 5, the Chair 
of the Committee on the Budget shall include in the Congressional Record an 
allocation contemplated by section 302(a) for the Committee on Appropriations 
for the remainder of fiscal year 2011 that assumes non-security spending at 
fiscal year 2008 levels or less.
