                               RESOLUTION


 
 To authorize the payment of legal expenses of Senate employees out of 
                   the contingent fund of the Senate.

    Resolved,

SECTION 1. AUTHORIZATION OF THE PAYMENT OF LEGAL EXPENSES.

    (a) In General.--The Committee on Rules and Administration is 
authorized to pay out of the contingent fund of the Senate the legal 
expenses incurred by Jean Manning and Erica Watkins for the employment 
of private counsel to represent them with respect to official actions 
and responsibilities before the grand jury in the United States 
District Court for the District of Columbia.
    (b) Determination.--The amount of expenses paid pursuant to 
subsection (a) shall be determined by the Committee on Rules and 
Administration.
