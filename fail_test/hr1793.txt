                                 A BILL


 
         To suspend temporarily the duty on Pyrithiobac Sodium.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. TEMPORARY SUSPENSION OF DUTY.

    (a) In General.--Subchapter II of chapter 99 of the Harmonized 
Tariff Schedule of the United States is amended by inserting in 
numerical sequence the following new heading:



``   9902.30.         Sodium 2-chloro-                                                                          
                       6-[(4,6-                                                                                 
                       dimethoxy                                                                                
                       pyrimidin-2-                                                                             
                       y1)thio]                                                                                 
                       benzoate                                                                                 
                       (pyrithiobac                                                                             
                       sodium)                                                                                  
                       2933.59.1000...  Free              No change         No change        On or before       
                                                                                             12/31/98         ''

    (b) Effective Date.--The amendment made by this section applies 
with respect to goods entered, or withdrawn from warehouse for 
consumption, on or after the 15th day of enactment of this Act.
