                                 A BILL


 
 To authorize the Secretary of the Interior to enter into an agreement 
 with the Arch Hurley Conservancy District in New Mexico, authorizing 
the District to prepay any amounts outstanding under water reclamation 
                          repayment contracts.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. PREPAYMENT OF CERTAIN WATER RECLAMATION REPAYMENT 
              OBLIGATIONS RELATING TO TUCUMCARI WATER RECLAMATION 
              PROJECT.

    The Secretary of the Interior may enter into an agreement with the 
Arch Hurley Conservancy District in New Mexico, authorizing the 
District to prepay any present value amounts outstanding under 
repayment contracts between the United States and the District with 
respect to the Tucumcari water reclamation project in San Miguel and 
Quay Counties, New Mexico.
