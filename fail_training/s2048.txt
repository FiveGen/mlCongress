                                 A BILL


 
            To provide for the elimination of duty on Ziram.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. ELIMINATION OF DUTY ON ZIRAM.

    (a) In General.--Subheading 3808.20.24 of the Harmonized Tariff 
Schedule of the United States is amended by striking ``and Metiram'' 
and inserting ``Metiram; and Ziram''.
    (b) Effective Date.--The amendment made by subsection (a) applies 
with respect to goods entered, or withdrawn from warehouse for 
consumption, on or after the 15th day after the date of the enactment 
of this Act.
