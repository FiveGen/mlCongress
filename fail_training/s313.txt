                                 A BILL


 
     To repeal a provision of the International Air Transportation 
Competition Act of 1979 relating to air transportation from Love Field, 
                                 Texas.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. REPEAL OF PROVISION RELATING TO LOVE FIELD, TEXAS.

    Section 29 of the International Air Transportation Competition Act 
of 1979 (94 Stat. 48) is repealed.
