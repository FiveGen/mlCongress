                                 A BILL


 
To amend title 38, United States Code, to extend to all members of the 
 Armed Forces eligible for educational assistance under the Montgomery 
   GI Bill the authority to transfer entitlement to such educational 
                       assistance to dependents.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. SHORT TITLE.

    This Act may be cited as the ``Expanding Education for Military 
Families Act of 2002''.

SEC. 2. TRANSFER OF ENTITLEMENT TO BASIC EDUCATIONAL ASSISTANCE FOR ALL 
              MEMBERS OF THE ARMED FORCES.

    (a) In General.--Subsection (b) of section 3020 of title 38, United 
States Code, is amended--
            (1) by striking paragraph (2);
            (2) by redesignating paragraph (3) as paragraph (2); and
            (3) by adding ``and'' at the end of paragraph (1).
    (b) Conforming Amendments.--(1) Such section is further amended--
            (A) in the heading, by striking ``: members of the Armed 
        Forces with critical military skills'';
            (B) in subsection (a), by striking ``with critical military 
        skills''; and
            (C) in subsection (i), by striking ``subsection (b)(3)'' 
        and inserting ``subsection (b)(2)''.
    (2) The item relating to section 3020 in the table of sections at 
the beginning of chapter 30 of such title is amended by striking the 
following:

``: members of the Armed Forces with critical military skills''.
