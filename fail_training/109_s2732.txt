                                 A BILL


 
         To suspend temporarily the duty on Pigment Yellow 219.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. PIGMENT YELLOW 219.

    (a) In General.--Subchapter II of chapter 99 of the Harmonized 
Tariff Schedule of the United States is amended by inserting in 
numerical sequence the following new heading:


``      9902.09.77      Pigment Yellow 219  Free         No change        No change        On or before 12/  ''.
                         (provided for in                                                   31/2009
                         subheading
                         3204.17.60)......

    (b) Effective Date.--The amendment made by subsection (a) applies 
to goods entered, or withdrawn from warehouse for consumption, on or 
after the 15th day after the date of enactment of this Act.
