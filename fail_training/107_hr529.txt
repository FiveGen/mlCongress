                                 A BILL


 
  To authorize the Secretary of Transportation to require the use of 
recycled materials in the construction of Federal-aid highway projects.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. AUTHORITY TO REQUIRE USE OF RECYCLED MATERIALS.

    The Secretary of Transportation may issue regulations requiring the 
use of recycled materials in the construction of any Federal-aid 
highway.
