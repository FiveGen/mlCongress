                                 A BILL


 
    To amend the Salton Sea Reclamation Act of 1998 to reauthorize 
activities relating to river reclamation and wetlands projects for the 
        Alamo River and New River, Imperial County, California.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. REAUTHORIZATION OF RIVER RECLAMATION AND WETLANDS PROJECTS, 
              ALAMO RIVER AND NEW RIVER, IMPERIAL COUNTY, CALIFORNIA.

    Section 201(d) of the Salton Sea Reclamation Act of 1998 (Public 
Law 105-372; 112 Stat. 3381) is amended by striking ``$3,000,000'' and 
inserting ``$30,000,000 for fiscal years after fiscal year 2002''.
