                                 A BILL


 
To extend the Nazi War Crimes and Japanese Imperial Government Records 
                 Interagency Working Group for 2 years.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. TWO-YEAR EXTENSION OF NAZI WAR CRIMES AND JAPANESE IMPERIAL 
              GOVERNMENT RECORDS INTERAGENCY WORKING GROUP.

    Section 802(b)(1) of the Japanese Imperial Government Disclosure 
Act of 2000 (5 U.S.C. 552 note; Public Law 106-567; 114 Stat. 2865) is 
amended by striking ``4 years'' and inserting ``6 years''.
