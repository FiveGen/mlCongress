                                 A BILL


 
    To extend the temporary suspension of duty on Isobornyl Acetate.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. ISOBORNYL ACETATE.

    (a) In General.--Heading 9902.29.71 of the Harmonized Tariff 
Schedule of the United States is amended in the effective period column 
by striking the date contained therein and inserting ``12/31/2006''.
    (b) Effective Date.--The amendment made by subsection (a) applies 
with respect to goods entered, or withdrawn from warehouse for 
consumption, on or after the 15th day after the date of the enactment 
of this Act.
