                                 A BILL


 
 To amend the Omnibus Parks and Public Lands Management Act of 1996 to 
 extend the legislative authority for the Black Patriots Foundation to 
                    establish a commemorative work.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. BLACK REVOLUTIONARY WAR PATRIOTS MEMORIAL.

    Section 506 of the Omnibus Parks and Public Lands Management Act of 
1996 (40 U.S.C. 1003 note; 110 Stat. 4155) is amended by striking 
``1998'' and inserting ``2003''.
