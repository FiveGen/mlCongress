                                 A BILL


 
         To repeal the Cuban Adjustment Act, Public Law 89-732.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. REPEAL OF THE CUBAN ADJUSTMENT ACT.

            (a) In General.--Public Law 89-732 is repealed.
            (b) Effective Date; Transition.--
                    (1) In general.--Subject to paragraph (2), the 
                repeal made by subsection (a) shall take effect on the 
                date of the enactment of this Act and shall apply to 
                any alien admitted or paroled into the United States on 
                or after the date of the enactment of this Act.
                    (2) Transition.--The repeal made by subsection (a) 
                shall not apply to any alien admitted or paroled into 
                the United States before the date of the enactment of 
                this Act.
