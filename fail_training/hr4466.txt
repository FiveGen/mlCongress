                                 A BILL


 
 To amend the Transportation Equity Act for the 21st Century to repeal 
 the Interstate System Reconstruction and Rehabilitation Pilot Program.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. INTERSTATE SYSTEM RECONSTRUCTION AND REHABILITATION PILOT 
              PROGRAM REPEALED.

    Section 1216(b) of the Transportation Equity Act for the 21st 
Century (112 Stat. 212-214) is repealed.
