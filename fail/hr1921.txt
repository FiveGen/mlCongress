                                 A BILL


 
       To suspend until January 1, 2001, the duty on a chemical.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. SUSPENSION OF DUTY.

    Subchapter II of chapter 99 of the Harmonized Tariff Schedule of 
the United States is amended by inserting in numerical sequence the 
following new item:

      

``    9902.32.14     1,3-                                                                                       
                      Naphthalenedisul                                                                          
                      fonic acid, 6-                                                                            
                      amino- (CAS No.                                                                           
                      118-33-2)                                                                                 
                      (provided for in                                                                          
                      subheading                                                                                
                      2921.45.90.90)..  Free              No change        No change        On or before 12/    
                                                                                             31/2000            
                                                                                                              ''

SEC. 2. APPLICABILITY.

    The amendment made by section 1 applies with respect to goods 
entered, or withdrawn from warehouse for consumption, on or after the 
15th day after the date of the enactment of this Act.
