To suspend until December 31, 2002, the duty on Butanamide, 2,2'-[1-2,-
ethanediylbis(oxy-2,1-phenylene-azo)]bis[N-(2,3-dihydro-2-oxo-1H-benz-

              ETHANEDIYLBIS(OXY-2,1-PHENYLENEAZO)] BIS[N-(2,3-DIHYDRO-
              2-OXO-1H-BENZIMIDAZOL-5-YL)-3-OXO-.

                                                                                              31/2002
