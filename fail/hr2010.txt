                                 A BILL


 
 To designate the bridge over lock and dam numbered 4 on the Arkansas 
          River as the ``Lawrence Blackwell Memorial Bridge''.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. DESIGNATION.

    The bridge over lock and dam numbered 4 on the Arkansas River, 
Arkansas, constructed as part of the project for navigation on the 
Arkansas River and tributaries, shall be known and designated as the 
``Lawrence Blackwell Memorial Bridge''.

SEC. 2. LEGAL REFERENCE.

    A reference in any law, regulation, document, or record of the 
United States to the bridge referred to in section 1 shall be deemed to 
be a reference to the ``Lawrence Blackwell Memorial Bridge''.
