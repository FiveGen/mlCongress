                                 AN ACT


 
To designate the United States Post Office located at 3813 Main Street 
in East Chicago, Indiana, as the ``Corporal Harold Gomez Post Office''.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. DESIGNATION.

    The United States Post Office located at 3813 Main Street in East 
Chicago, Indiana, shall be known and designated as the ``Corporal 
Harold Gomez Post Office''.

SEC. 2. REFERENCES.

    Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, paper, or other 
record of the United States to the post office referred to in section 1 
shall be deemed to be a reference to the ``Corporal Harold Gomez Post 
Office''.

            Passed the House of Representatives October 7, 1998.
