                               RESOLUTION


 
To authorize representation by the Senate Legal Counsel in the case of 
                        Lannak v, Biden, et al.

Whereas, in the case of Lannak v. Biden, et al., No. 06-CV-0180, pending in the 
        United States District Court for the District of Delaware, the plaintiff 
        has named as defendants Senators Joseph R. Biden, Jr. and Thomas R. 
        Carper; and
Whereas, pursuant to sections 703(a) and 704(a)(1) of the Ethics in Government 
        Act of 1978, 2 U.S.C. 288b(a) and 288c(a)(1), the Senate may direct its 
        counsel to defend Members, officers, and employees of the Senate in 
        civil actions relating to their official responsibilities: Now, 
        therefore, be it
    Resolved, That the Senate Legal Counsel is authorized to represent 
Senators Joseph R. Biden, Jr. and Thomas R. Carper in the case of 
Lannak v. Biden, et al.
                                 <all>
