    Resolved, That upon adoption of this resolution it shall be in order to 
consider the further conference report to accompany the bill (H.R. 3010) making

Education, and Related Agencies for the fiscal year ending September 30, 2006, 
and for other purposes. All points of order against the conference report and 
against its consideration are waived. The conference report shall be considered 
as read.
