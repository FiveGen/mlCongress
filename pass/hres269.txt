[H. Res. 269 Engrossed in House (EH)]


                 In the House of Representatives, U.S.,

                                                      October 22, 1997.
    Resolved, That upon the adoption of this resolution it shall be in order to 
consider in the House the joint resolution (H.J. Res. 97) making further 
continuing appropriations for the fiscal year 1998, and for other purposes. The 
joint resolution shall be considered as read for amendment. The joint resolution 
shall be debatable for one hour equally divided and controlled by the chairman 
and ranking minority member of the Committee on Appropriations. The previous 
question shall be considered as ordered on the joint resolution to final passage 
without intervening motion except one motion to recommit.
            Attest:

                                                                          Clerk.
